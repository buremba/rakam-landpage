How Rakam Works

  • Connect to your database
  • Map your data layout
  • Start creating reports
  • Share with your teams!

Connect to your database

We support most of the data warehouses that is commonly used for storing event-data, you simply enter your database credentials and connect to your data. It works similar to the other Business Intelligence tools.

Register and connect your database

Map your data layout

You define the event tables using our user interface, the user attribute and timestamp columns in event tables so that we can provide you the product analytics tool for non-technical users to analyze your event data.

Create reports & dashboards

Explore your data with funnel, retention and segmentation reports or directly using SQL for custom reports. You can also create dashboards with custom layout from your reports.

Learn More

Share with your teams

You can share your reports & dashboards with your teams easily. We provide enterprise-level features such as table, row and column permissions and support third-party identity providers such as OKTA.

How is Rakam different?

  • We have similar concept of LookML with Jsonnet but we mainly focus on customer data and behavioral analytics whereas Looker is a more generic tool.

    Learn more →

  • Unlike Mixpanel, we do not require you to send the data to our services. If you already have your customer data in your database, Rakam connects to your database and provide similar reporting features.

    Learn more →

  • Tableau focuses on charts and dashboards whereas Rakam is more of an ad-hoc data exploration tool. We help you to understand how your users behave and we provide high level features for this use-case.

    Learn more →

  • GA provides pre-defined reports mostly for websites but we're able to provide the full customer journey from websites, mobile apps, email, CRM and much more if you're able to collect the data in your database.

    Learn more →
